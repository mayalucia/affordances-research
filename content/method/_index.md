## Why this section exists

A research artefact that does not name its method is not yet
finished. The questions a reviewer should be able to answer after
reading **Theory** include not just *is the math right* but *was
the right thing built*, *is the right thing left undone*, and *did
the work get reviewed honestly*.

This section answers those questions explicitly.

## What is here

- **Three phases** — why we split commitment into Phase 1, Phase
  1.5, Phase 2; what each phase costs and what it earns.
- **Disciplines** — the cold-read pass; the two-register split
  (framing vs methods); the attribution-honesty rules.
- **Deferred** — what we name but do not build, and why deferring
  is part of the work, not an apology.
- **Coalition** — how the work was reviewed: cite-or-strike passes,
  frame-correction propagation, the four-spirit audit. *(Full
  walkthrough lands post-May-15.)*

## Read order

Each subsection stands alone. Read in any order. If you read only
one, read **Three phases** — it is the spine.
